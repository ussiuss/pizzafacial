About Us

We are pizzafacial, a band that loves to make music and eat pizza. Our journey started in 2023 and we've been rocking ever since.

We are not just any ordinary band. We are a group of passionate musicians who are dedicated to creating unique and memorable music experiences for our fans.

With our energetic performances and catchy tunes, we aim to bring joy and excitement to every stage we step on. Whether it's a small intimate venue or a large music festival, we always give it our all.

So join us on this musical journey and let's rock together!
